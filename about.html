About Us

Our app is dedicated to raising awareness about endangered species and promoting wildlife conservation. Through 3D scanning and augmented reality, users can explore and learn about these animals in a captivating way. We provide a platform for users to access comprehensive information, make direct donations to conservation organizations, and engage with educational content, fostering a community of like-minded individuals committed to safeguarding our planet's precious wildlife

Animals that we protect (so far)

  • Asian Elephant
  • Bornean Orangutan
  • Giant Panda
  • Emperor Penguin
  • Polar Bear
  • Javan Rhinoceros
