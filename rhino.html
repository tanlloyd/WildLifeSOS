Javan Rhinoceros

Javan rhinoceros has a distinctive, armor-like skin with deep folds, and it primarily inhabits dense tropical forests in Southeast Asia

Discover more about me

Play to hear me shout!

Why am I endangered?

1. Destruction and fragmentation of my tropical forest habitat

2. I am hunted by poachers for my valuable horns

3. I have slow reproduction rate, with long gestation periods and infrequent births

How to save me?

1. Contribute to reputable wildlife conservation organizations that protects me

2. Educate others about my plight and the importance of preserving my habitat and stopping poaching

3. Avoid purchasing products made from my horn
