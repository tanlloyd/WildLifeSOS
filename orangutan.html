Bornean Orangutan

The Bornean orangutan is known for its distinctive reddish-brown fur and remarkable agility in the trees, where it spends most of its life. These great apes are highly intelligent and have a unique, thoughtful demeanor, making them fascinating creatures of the rainforests.

Why am I endangered?

1. Deforestation, as vast areas of my habitat are being cleared for palm oil plantations and agriculture

2. The remaining forests are increasingly fragmented, isolating my populations and limiting my ability to find food and mates

3. I am victim of illegal wildlife trade, with infants being captured and sold as pets or for the entertainment industry

How to save me?

1. Choose products made with sustainable palm oil or palm oil alternatives

2. Never buy or support the sale of orangutans as pets or in the entertainment industry

3. Contribute to and volunteer with organizations working to protect the Bornean orangutan and my habitat, and spread awareness about my conservation needs
