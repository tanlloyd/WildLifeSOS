Asian Elephant

Asian elephants are distinguished by their smaller ears compared to African elephants. They have a gentle and sociable nature, often living in close-knit family groups.

Why am I endangered?

1. Habitat loss due to human activities like deforestation and urban development

2. Illegal poaching for my ivory, meat, and body parts

3. Inadequate conservation efforts and insufficient protection of my habitats

How to save me?

1. Support organizations working to protect and conserve my habitats, such as national parks and wildlife reserves

2. Promote responsible and ethical tourism practices, avoiding activities that exploit me for entertainment or riding

3. Educate others about the importance of Asian elephant conservation and spread awareness about my endangered status
