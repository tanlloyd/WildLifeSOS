Polar Bear

Polar bears are large, carnivorous mammals uniquely adapted to their Arctic habitat, with thick fur and blubber for insulation and powerful limbs for swimming. Their white fur and keen sense of smell help them hunt seals, making them iconic symbols of the frozen North.

Why am I endangered?

1. Melting sea ice due to rising global temperatures is reducing the my land mass for home

2. Pollutants like PCBs and other toxins accumulate in the Arctic ecosystem

3. Illegal hunting and poaching of me for my fur, body parts, or as trophies

How to save me?

1. Contribute to organizations working to protect me by donating or volunteering

2. Everyday actions like conserving energy, using public transportation, and supporting renewable energy sources can help combat climate change

3. Avoid products made from my parts and support conservation organizations working to protect me
